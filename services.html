Service Categories

Four types of AI solutions

Every AI solution we build falls into one of these categories. Which one fits depends on what you're actually trying to fix.

💬

AI Chatbots & Assistants

Conversational AI embedded in your website, booking system, or internal tools. Available around the clock to answer questions, qualify leads, handle bookings, and hand off to your team when a human is needed.

  • Customer-facing booking assistants
  • Lead qualification chatbots
  • Internal FAQ and knowledge base bots
  • After-hours support agents
⚙️

Workflow Automation

Connect your existing tools with AI-powered triggers and actions. The goal is eliminating hours of repetitive work each week without replacing the systems your team already relies on.

  • Invoice follow-up automation
  • Lead routing and CRM updates
  • Email drafting and scheduling pipelines
  • Supplier price monitoring
📄

Document & Content AI

AI that reads, summarises, drafts, and generates documents. Feed it your notes and get polished output in seconds. Useful anywhere you're producing the same kinds of things over and over.

  • Listing and product description writers
  • Contract and report summarisers
  • Review response generators
  • Client update email drafters
📊

Data Processing & Reporting

AI that runs on a schedule, processes invoices, leads, or inventory reports, and delivers plain-language briefings or triggers the next action. Back-office automation that doesn't need supervision.

  • Daily inventory alert summaries
  • Batch invoice processing
  • Scheduled performance briefings
  • Abandoned cart recovery campaigns
Technology

The right AI model for the job

We don't default to one platform. We pick the model that fits your use case, your budget, and your data requirements.

GPT-4o
Best All-Round

Great for complex reasoning, customer-facing chat, document analysis, and content generation. Our default for most client-facing applications.

Claude (Anthropic)
Long Documents

Excellent for long-document analysis, nuanced professional writing, and safety-sensitive applications. Strong extended context window.

Gemini
Multimodal

Strong with text, image, and audio together. A natural fit for clients already in the Google Workspace ecosystem.

Open Source
Privacy-First

Llama, Mistral, and others. Best for clients with strict data privacy requirements or on-premise needs. No per-token API costs after deployment.

Specialist Models
Domain-Specific

Purpose-built models for legal, medical, or financial industries where fine-tuned accuracy in a regulated field is critical.

Integration Platforms

How we connect AI to your existing tools

Most AI solutions need to plug into something, whether that's your booking system, CRM, email, or accounting software. Here's what we typically use and why.

Make (Integromat)
Primary automation platform

Our default for most clients. Visual, reliable, and connects to 1,000+ apps. Works really well for SMB workflow automation.

Zapier
Simpler automations

Used when the client already has a Zapier subscription or when the use case is simple enough that Make would be overkill.

n8n
Self-hosted option

Open-source automation for clients who want full control and self-hosted workflows. Requires more technical setup.

Voiceflow / Botpress
Chatbot builders

Purpose-built conversation designers. Used when a visual conversation flow speeds up delivery on chatbot projects.

Custom Python / API
When no platform fits

When no no-code platform supports the integration we need, or when performance and flexibility are critical.

Railway / Render
Lightweight cloud hosting

Simple cloud hosting for lightweight custom applications and APIs. Low cost, easy to deploy, easy to maintain.
