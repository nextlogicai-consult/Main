Being Upfront

We're not the right fit for everyone

We'd rather say that now than waste both our time.

Good fit if...

  • You have a specific, repeatable problem costing you time every week
  • Your team is willing to learn and adapt to new tools
  • You want a long-term partner, not a manual and a goodbye
  • You'd rather start small and prove value before scaling
  • You care more about something that works than something that looks impressive

Probably not if...

  • You need something built in 48 hours with no planning
  • Your data is completely disorganized and you're not ready to fix that
  • You want to hand off a problem and never think about it again
  • You're shopping purely on price regardless of outcome
  • You need something outside our core service areas
Common Questions

Things people ask before booking

If something is stopping you from picking a time, the answer is probably here.

Do I need to know anything about AI before the call?

Not at all. We work with people who have never touched an AI tool and with people who've tried a few things and gotten stuck. We start from where you are.

What if I'm not sure AI is even the right solution?

That's one of the most common things we hear. Figuring out whether AI is the right tool is half the point of the call. Sometimes it is. Sometimes a simpler fix makes more sense — and we'll tell you that directly.

Is this actually free or is there a catch?

Genuinely free. No credit card, no trial, nothing to cancel. We do discovery calls because they're the only honest way to figure out if there's a real fit.

How quickly can we get started after the call?

If there's a clear fit, a project brief is usually in front of you within 2–3 days. Starter projects run 1–2 weeks and Builder projects run 2–4 weeks from signed brief to launch.

What does it cost?

We don't publish fixed prices because scope varies too much for that to be honest. We price on value delivered, not hours spent. You'll have full pricing in writing before anything gets started.

What if I just want to ask a quick question first?

Email us at info@nextlogicai.com and we'll get back to you within 4 business hours. No question is too basic.
